About Me

My name is Luc and I am a young designer who has recently graduated with a Bachelor of Design at the Australian National University. This degree took a broad holistic approach to design which instead of specialising gives students a range of diverse design skills. This degree has provided me with a range of skills associated with both the industrial design area, as well as graphic design, UI, and design theory. I am very passionate about my personal art and creative practice as well as outdoor sports such as hiking, mountain biking, and snowboarding. I recently graduated, leaving a role on the visual team at the ANU Observer. Here I worked as a graphic designer with expectations of professional quality visuals with fast turnarounds.
